Ofri Yehuda - PRD Components (from Medium/Design Bootcamp)

Sources examined: Product School, Aha!, Atlassian, Monday, Nira templates, lastartup.co.il

Recurring components:
- Requirements
- Assumptions (key ideas that determine project direction)
- Success criteria (goals and measurement system)
- Project overview (connect team to purpose)

Additional useful components:
- Scoping (boundaries, limitations, prevent scope creep)
- User flows and Wireframes
- Data requirements
- Milestones and Timeline (Gantt example)

Note: Article references Google Doc template; we also have the Word version locally. No single correct PRD format; ideal fit depends on PM and product (product line vs new feature vs first product, technical vs consumer-facing).
