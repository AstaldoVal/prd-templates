monday.com PRD Template - 10 Components

1. Project overview and background
   - Product purpose, target users, strategic context, market analysis, competitive positioning

2. Objectives and success metrics
   - Product goals, measurable KPIs for impact and alignment

3. Stakeholders, roles, and responsibilities
   - Team members, decision-makers, contributors, owners per requirement/deliverable

4. User personas, stories, and use cases
   - Target users, needs, core scenarios, user stories, real-world context

5. Functional requirements
   - Essential features and behaviors, priorities, linked diagrams/wireframes/process flows

6. Non-functional requirements
   - Performance, reliability, scalability, security, localization, compliance

7. Assumptions, dependencies, and constraints
   - External systems, preconditions, limitations, project assumptions

8. Timeline, milestones, and deliverables
   - Major phases, deadlines, expected outputs

9. Design resources and wireframes
   - Mockups, diagrams, workflow charts for complex requirements

10. Out-of-scope items and open questions
    - Intentional exclusions, outstanding questions for team discussion

Template completion (6 sections):
