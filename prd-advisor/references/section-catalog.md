# PRD Section Catalog

Brief descriptions of sections used across templates. Use when explaining what belongs in a section or when the user asks "what goes in X?"

---

## Problem & Context

- **Problem Statement** — Who has the problem, what it is, evidence it exists (data, research)
- **Background** — Context, personas, customer interviews, market opportunity
- **High Level Approach** — Short summary of intended approach, not full solution
- **Vision Statement** — Where the product/company aims to be
- **Product Origin** — Idea source, pilot studies, pivotal moments

---

## Goals & Metrics

- **Goals (Measurable)** — Objectives with baselines and targets
- **Goals (Immeasurable)** — How it should feel, qualitative outcomes
- **Success Criteria** — KPIs, benchmarks, definition of done
- **North Star Metric** — The one metric that matters most
- **Secondary Metrics** — Supporting metrics
- **Guardrail Metrics** — Metrics to watch (e.g., don't degrade X)
- **Input Metrics** — Leading indicators that drive output (e.g., # who select topic, # who pin)

---

## Scope & Alignment

- **Non-goals** — What's explicitly out of scope and why
- **Solution Alignment** — What's In, What's Out, Open Questions
- **Out of Scope** — Boundaries, limitations, future iterations
- **Strategic Alignment** — How this supports business/product goals

---

## Narrative & Empathy

- **Narrative** — Story of user challenge, how solution helps, positive result
- **Before/After Stories** — User state before vs after
- **Common Use Case** — Typical scenario
- **Edge Case** — Unusual but important scenario

---

## Solution & Requirements

- **Requirements** — What the product must do; user stories with P0/P1
- **Functional Requirements** — Detailed feature list, grouped by priority
- **Key Features (Plan of Record)** — Feature | Priority | Description | Size estimate
- **Acceptance Criteria** — Gherkin-style (Given/When/Then) or bullet list
- **General Product Behavior** — High-level behavior, optional user stories
- **Key Logic** — Business rules, edge case handling, scenarios

---

## Impact & Sizing

- **Impact Sizing Model** — Step-by-step calculation, revenue/bottom-line impact, confidence, assumptions
- **Future Considerations** — Planned follow-ups, build-now implications

---

## User Experience

- **User Experience (UX)** — Flow, design link, usability
- **Key Flows** — Screen-by-screen walkthroughs, empty and error states
- **User Flows / Task Flows** — Decision → Action → Display
- **Wireframes** — Visual representation of screens
- **UI/UX Highlights** — Key interaction patterns

---

## Technical

- **Technical Requirements** — Tech stack, architecture, security, performance
- **Technical Considerations** — APIs, data, integration points
- **Data Requirements** — What to collect, format, storage
- **Data Storage & Privacy** — Where data lives, compliance
- **Integration Points** — Existing systems, third parties
- **Non-Functional Requirements** — Speed, security, scalability

---

## Launch & Execution

- **Launch Plan** — Phases, experiment design (Success criteria, Eligibility, Test, Control, Ramp), Risks & Dependencies
- **Key Milestones** — Date, owner, status, dependencies
- **Meeting Notes** — Discussions and decisions captured in PRD

---

## Supporting

- **Appendix** — Research, competitive analysis, supporting data
- **Open Questions** — Unresolved items for async discussion
- **Assumptions** — What we assume to be true; validation approach
- **Dependencies** — Technical, team, external
- **Risks** — Likelihood, impact, mitigation
